About TSwiftly

Do you consider yourself a mega Taylor Swift fan? Do you use the word “era” to describe your basic life scenarios? Do you have Taylor Swift as your top artist on your Wrapped each year? Then TSwiftly is the website for you! TSwiftly was created by Swifties, for Swifties, and is driven by Spotify API data. Using statistics such as tempo, energy, and danceability, TSwiftly can find you Taylor Swift songs based on user input on: Taylor's Version, Era, Duration, Tempo, Energy, and Popularity. We are working hard to implement the Similar Song feature, which will let users find the Taylor Swift song most similar to any given song on Spotify. Users also have the ability to click through their favorite songs, rating them against each other while two are on the screen. They can also skip songs if they cannot pick a favorite of the pair! The user can also rate Taylor's 14 albums. In live time, the user popularity data for albums and songs is projected on the landing page of TSwiftly.
